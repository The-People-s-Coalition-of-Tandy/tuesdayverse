Tuesday's Universe About
“Tuesday’s Universe” is a multi-media on-line installation of Tuesday Hamilton’s digital paintings.

This website is published, maintained, and distributed by the People’s Coalition of Tandy.

Tuesday Hamilton (Brooklyn, NY) and members from the Web Division of the People’s Coalition of Tandy are proud to deliver this new type of media-web-experience.
Each Painting has its own “Digital Gallery Room” (D.G.R.).
Each D.G.R. has a unique soundtrack: bespoke ambience inspired by the Painting, as if the viewer was in a physical space custom-made and curated to elevate the viewing experience.
We are forever grateful to the artists who supplied the audio: Mobéy Lola Irizarry, Julie, Ko T. C., Starla Online, Swaya, and elie!
